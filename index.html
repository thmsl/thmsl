What is Blockpass?

Blockpass is a safe passport for a connected world

Everything will be connected on blockchain.

Individuals, companies, devices and objects.

But we need a way to verify statements made by persons or things.

This is the role of Blockpass.

Blockpass is an on-chain device and human verification oracle.

We are the onchain Oracle for Know Your Client (KYC) and Know Your Device (KYD).

Ask Blockpass if an address has been verified and it will always give an answer.

How does it work?

Customers that want to do KYC download the Blockpass app and do KYC. They are then verified and can access any provider that accepts Blockpass.

For devices we provide a KYD service. If you are manufacturing objects you can register them for the first time on Blockpass. If you want to re-sell an object you can add them to Blockpass and we will verify the object.
