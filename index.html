ISSUES

The Connected World needs an identity system that support the verification of humans (KYC), object (KYO) and connected devices (KYD)

Identity verification requires each time an individual access for a new product or service, resulting in a slow and painful process that risks to reduce conversions.

Service Providers spend time and money developing internal KYC solutions and theyre exposed to compliance risk and 100% assumed liability.

IoT device vulnerability to hacks and device thefts may compromise data integrity and manipulation of confidential informations and participation in DDoS attacks.

Identity verification on networks is exposed to cyber attacks, ransomware, identity fraud and because of an increasingly stringent regulatory environment it is becoming harder and pricier for businesses to comply quickly and effectively.

MISSION

The goal of Blockpass is global realization of identity for the Internet of Everything

Through the use of blockchain technology and smart contracts, Blockpass is a production ready Regtech platform offering shared regulatory and compliance services for humans, companies, objects and devices.
This will enable the development of new applications that rely on a trusted connection between human and device identities.

Service provider cost reduction when on-boarding new customers

Customer inconvenience reduction when signing up for multiple blockchain service providers

Permit connected devices to transmit data into the blockchain as trusted Oracles
