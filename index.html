VISION

The "Connected World" needs an identity system that supports the verification of humans (KYC), objects (KYO) and connected devices (KYD) because of the following reasons :

IoT device vulnerability to hacks and compromised data integrity.

Interception or manipulation of confidential informations.
Participation in DDoS attacks.
Device thefts.

Increasingly stringent regulatory environment.

As governments vote in new regulations every year, it is becoming harder and pricier for businesses to comply quickly and effectively.

Identity verification required each time an individual accesses a new product or service.

Slow and painful.

Reduces conversions.

Service Providers spend time and money developing internal KYC solutions.

Slow and expensive.
Compliance risk.
100% assumed liability.

Identity verification on networks is difficult.

Cyber attacks, ransomware, identity fraud, are increasing globally.

MISSION

The goal of Blockpass is global realization of identity for the Internet of Everything. Through the use of blockchain technology and smart contracts, Blockpass is a production ready Regtech platform offering shared regulatory and compliance services for humans, companies, objects and devices. This will enable the development of new applications that rely on a trusted connection between human and device identities.

The Blockpass identity protocol will do the following

Service provider cost reduction when on-boarding new customers.
Customer inconvenience reduction when signing up for multiple blockchain service providers.
Allow individuals to transfer ownership, share or permission physical devices and objects.
Permit connected devices to transmit data into the blockchain as trusted Oracles.
Enable dynamic interaction with connected devices and Blockpass users.

How does it work?

Customers who want to perform KYC download the Blockpass mobile app.
They are then verified and can access any provider that accepts Blockpass.

For devices we provide a KYD service.
If you are manufacturing objects you can register them for the first time on Blockpass.
If you want to re-sell an object you can add it to Blockpass and we will verify it.

Humans

Download Blockpass mobile app.
Create an identity.
Complete the KYC process.
Register any service provider that accepts Blockpass.

Objects

Access the Object Manager Service in BlockPass app.
Create object identity.
Submit object document to Blockpass for verification.
Claim ownership, re-sell or share object with Blockpass verified Users.
